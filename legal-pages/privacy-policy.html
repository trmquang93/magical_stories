5. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our Commitment to Children's Privacy

We take special care to protect children's privacy and comply with COPPA requirements:

5.1 Information from Children Under 13

  • No Personal Information Collection: We do not collect names, addresses, or contact information from children
  • Story Content Only: We only collect story preferences and generated content
  • Parental Control: Parents can review and delete their child's information
  • No Behavioral Tracking: We do not track children across websites or apps

5.2 Parental Rights

Parents have the right to:

  • Review their child's personal information
  • Request deletion of their child's information
  • Refuse further collection of their child's information
  • Control their child's participation in activities

5.3 Parental Controls

  • Content filtering settings
  • Usage time limits
  • Age-appropriate content selection
  • Access code protection for settings
